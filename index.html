My services

Bharatanatyam

Originating from Tamil Nadu, Bharatanatyam is one of the oldest classical dance forms of India. It combines intricate footwork, graceful hand gestures (mudras), and expressive storytelling based on Hindu mythology.

Kathak

Kathak comes from North India and is known for its fast spins, rhythmic footwork, and storytelling through facial expressions. It often depicts tales from epics like Mahabharata and Ramayana.

Kuchipudi

Originating in Andhra Pradesh, Kuchipudi blends graceful movements with dramatic storytelling. Performers often enact mythological stories and sometimes include spoken dialogue along with dance.

Odissi

From Odisha, Odissi is characterized by fluid movements, sculpturesque poses, and devotional themes. It is deeply rooted in temple traditions and often portrays stories of Lord Jagannath and Krishna.

Kathakali

A vibrant dance-drama from Kerala, Kathakali is famous for its elaborate costumes, painted faces, and powerful expressions. It narrates stories from Hindu epics through intense gestures and music.

Manipuri

From Manipur, this dance form is gentle and lyrical, often depicting themes of devotion and love for Lord Krishna. It uses soft, flowing movements and colorful costumes.
